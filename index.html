Lodging Options

Hotels, AirBnb

There are a few hotels in nearby Puyallup and South Tacoma. Puyallup has a delightful downtown area with good restaurants. See below for our hotel suggestions. AirBnb is also an option, and there are a number of places available around our house ranging from private rooms to entire homes.

Camping at Our House

We have plenty of space in our backyard to accommodate a few campers! You'll need to supply your own gear, but you will have access to the ammenities of our house, like the bathroom and kitchen. Note that our neighborhood is not the quietest when you're sleeping outside; our neighbors have farm animals, and sometimes there is road noise. But our backyard is fully fenced and very private. Please let us know in your RSVP if you would like to camp at our house.
